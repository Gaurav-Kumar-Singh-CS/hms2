Acropolis of Athens

In classical times, the owl was the emblem of the city of Athens. It was the sacred bird of the goddess Athena, the city's patron deity. Vase paintings, statues and reliefs often show Athena in the company of an owl, which sometimes flies around her and at other times sits on her shoulder, imparting its counsel.

Athena
